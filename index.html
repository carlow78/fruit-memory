Rules of the Game


  1. To Start the game click on 1 card to turn it over.
  2. Click on another card, if it matches. Well done you have found a pair.
  3. If not both cards will be turned back over till you find a matching pair.
  4. There are 8 fruit cards to match to complete the game.
  5. Try to beat your quickest time or least amount of moves.
Good Luck and enjoy!
